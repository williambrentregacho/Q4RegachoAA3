OINK: - Olfactory Inhalation Kit is a portable herbal steam inhalation product made for relieving nasal congestion and helping users feel more comfortable. It uses pre-measured herbal packs with rice and natural herbs like oregano, ginger, and lemongrass. It is designed to be affordable, easy to use, and convenient for people who want a traditional tuob-inspired option. The kit is meant for supportive relief, not as a medical cure.
